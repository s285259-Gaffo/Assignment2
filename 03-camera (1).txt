Camera parameters
n Detection range, it is the distance that a camera can detect an object
n Angular resolution, it is the ability to distinguish details of an object
n Field of view, it is the extent of the observable world that is seen at any given moment

Camera parameters

n Focal length, distance between the lens and the image sensor when the subject is in focus
